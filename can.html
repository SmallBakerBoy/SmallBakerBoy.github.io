Robust Design

Our Can is a fat cow which is in a 4 part wave function due to two beam splitters working in colaberation with big math operatives

Sustainable Materials

Our Can is exclusively created through sustainable and renewable materials. We chose PLA due to its ease of recycling and green source.

Modular Design

Our Can uses friction fits and removable bolts to secure the two parts, this means that if there is any damage we can easily swap out the affected section instead of replacing the entire can.

Compact Components

Our internal components such as the pcb show have been designed to reduce the amount of space used.
