  FOREST SHOT  
"The Earth is what we all have in common" Wendell Berry

Welcome to Forest Shot, where innovation takes flight to protect the world’s forests. Our satellite technology utilises artificial intelligence to provide real-time monitoring of forest ecosystems, enabling the early detection of deforestation, illegal logging, and environmental threats. Forests are vital to our planet—they store carbon, sustain biodiversity, and regulate global climates. By leveraging modern technology, we can safeguard these precious resources, ensuring their health and resilience for future generations. Together, we can use the power of science to preserve the lungs of our Earth and secure a greener, more sustainable future.
